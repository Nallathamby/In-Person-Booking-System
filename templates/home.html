Welcome to Rogers Admin Portal

This tool is designed exclusively for Rogers administrators to efficiently manage customer bookings, technician assignments, services, appointments, and feedback.

What You Can Do:

  • Manage Customers: Create, update, delete, and view customer information.
  • Assign Technicians: Handle technician records, expertise, and availability.
  • Set Services: Define and update WiFi-related services such as installation, troubleshooting, and optimization.
  • Schedule Appointments: Book and track appointments for customers with available technicians.
  • Collect Feedback: Monitor customer feedback and satisfaction for ongoing improvements.

About This Portal:

Rogers Admin Portal streamlines the management of WiFi-related services and support. Whether it's setting up a new connection or troubleshooting issues, this tool empowers administrators to provide top-notch service while efficiently organizing records and schedules.
